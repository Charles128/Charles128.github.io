Movie Ninja

Survey Monkey for Movies. Ever had to organize a trip to the movies with your friends? This app creates a custom movie survey pre-populated with information (runtime, synopsis, etc.) from the Rotten Tomatoes API. Users are then given a custom survey URL that can be sent to friends who can in turn vote on the movies they want to see.
